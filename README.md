# 📊 End-to-End E-Commerce Sales Analytics Project

An end-to-end Data Analytics project that analyzes the **Superstore Sales (2015–2018)** dataset to uncover sales trends, customer behavior, product performance, and regional insights. The project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow using **Python, MySQL, and Power BI**.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The objective of this project is to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights. The project covers every stage of the data analytics lifecycle, from data cleaning and SQL analysis to dashboard development and business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The company needs a clear understanding of its sales performance to support business decisions. This project answers key business questions related to:

- Total Sales and Orders
- Average Order Value (AOV)
- Sales Trends
- Product Performance
- Customer Analysis
- Regional Performance
- Seasonal Sales Trends
- Top & Low Performing Products

## 🔍 Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales among all categories.
- The West region recorded the highest sales.
- The South region showed the lowest sales performance.
- Consumer customers contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Sales peaked during November and December, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- Overall sales increased between 2015 and 2018, despite a temporary decline in 2016.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Improve sales performance in the South region.
- Increase inventory before the holiday season.
- Promote high-performing products.
- Review low-performing products for optimization.
- Investigate the decline in sales during 2016.
